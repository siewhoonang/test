SP Online Parents' Forum 2022

This year, SP Parents Forum is moving online!

Conducted on Zoom, the webinars will be held from 1 June 2022 to 30 June 2022.

SP parents' forum is an informative session where parents are invited to get to know more about Singapore Polytechnic, the different schools within the institution and the range of courses offered through talks hosted. Parents can learn more about how the classes are carried out, the teaching styles, and how their child can benefit from Singapore Poly’s education. Through the parents' forum, parents can gain a better understanding and thus make a more informed decision with their child on their education path.



Parents' Forum Zoom Webinar Schedule

Parents whose children are looking to enrol in SP, and students, can understand more about what SP has to offer and its culture.
